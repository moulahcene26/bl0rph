So this is the first post.

I've kept notes for years, scattered across text files, Obsidian vaults,
and the margins of Burp projects. Most of it never leaves my machine, which
is a waste, because the notes are usually more honest than the reports.

This is me trying to do it in the open.

## what ends up here

- **findings**: the interesting things I run into while researching,
  written up once it's fair to share them. the whole chain, dead ends included.
- **techniques**: small things that worked and are worth remembering.
- **thoughts**: half-formed ideas, questions, and things I'm still chewing on.

## a couple of rules i'm setting for myself

1. nothing goes up before it's fixed and it's ok to talk about.
2. the dead ends stay in. the path matters more than the result.
3. plain markdown, plain html, no build step. if it needs a framework, it
   doesn't belong here.

> Write down the process, not just the payoff.

That's it. Posts show up on the front page as I write them. More soon.
